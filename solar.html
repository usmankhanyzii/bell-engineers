On-Grid Solar System

On-Grid systems are designed to connect with the utility grid and allow load sharing by solar energy and grid. This system may not need to produce 100% of the electricity demand and its works to supplement the utility grid to power the loads.

Off-Grid Solar System

Off-Grid systems are designed to make your home an independant power plant. You will be fully disconnected from Grid and system will be running on batteries when solar isn't available

Hybrid Solar System

These systems are quite sophisticated. They are fully programmable, which provides a combination of all the energy sources available; solar, Grid and battery are connected independently to power essential load
